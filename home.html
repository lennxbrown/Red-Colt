The Project

We are integrating our knowledge of coding and systems to creat a Raspberry Pi that is able to take measurments in the Morningside University Greenhouse. It will also be able to send said measurments to this website for easy access to those that need it.

Services and Materials Used

  • Thonny (MicroPython)
  • Python Flask
  • Raspberry Pi Pico W
  • Arducam Camera
